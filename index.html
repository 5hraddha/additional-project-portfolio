Who I Am

My name is Shraddha. I am currently a student at Practicum by Yandex, learning to become a web developer.

I am currently a homemaker interested in exploring the field of web development. Having worked as a Data Warehouse Engineer for close to 5 years, felt being limited by business standards and scopes to implement any of my creative ideas, so I always nurtured a keen interest to Web Development as it was alluring, captivating and always one that needs a creative touch! I certainly believe that it's one of the ideal escape routes and a perfect excuse for me to reignite my love for art alongside painting the web with my ideas and colors, and also getting paid for it makes it a worthwhile effort, and I love it!!

I am a beginner in HTML, CSS and Javascript and understand that learning only the techniques is not sufficient. I have always wanted a guidance and support through which I can mature in approaching the problem and having a clear understanding of why I am doing what I am doing. While self-learning, I managed to learn in bits and pieces but there was no cohesiveness. I am grateful for Practicum's support system (tutors, reviewers and fellow students) and looking forward to learn together.
